Before You Reach Out

A few things people usually ask

— What should I have ready before contacting you?

Your most recent academic transcript, a copy of your passport, and a sense of what you'd like to study — everything else we help you gather.

— Do you help with both China and Georgia applications?

Yes — we'll walk you through the differences between the two so you can choose the destination that fits your goals and budget.

— How long does it take to hear back from you?

Telegram messages are usually answered within a few hours on weekdays; email within a day.

— Is there a cost just to ask questions?

No — an initial conversation about your options is always free.
