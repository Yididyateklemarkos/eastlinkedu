Who We Are

A steady hand through an unfamiliar system

Applying to a Chinese university from Ethiopia means navigating a different language, a different academic calendar, and paperwork that rarely matches what you already have. EastLink Edu exists to close that gap.

We work directly with students and their families to identify the right university and program in China or Georgia, prepare a complete and accurate application, and follow it through admission, scholarship review, and visa approval — so nothing stalls because of a missing form or a missed deadline.

Where You Can Study

Two countries, one clear process

China and Georgia both offer strong academic programs, affordable tuition, and growing scholarship access for international students — we help you weigh both before you commit.

🇨🇳

China

Home to over 50 of our partner universities, from globally ranked research institutions to specialized technical schools, with strong government scholarship coverage for eligible students.

  • Bachelor's, Master's & PhD placements
  • HSK / Mandarin bridge programs
  • Full and partial scholarship tracks
🇬🇪

Georgia

A fast-growing option for Ethiopian students — English-taught programs, EU-adjacent academic standards, and a considerably shorter, simpler visa process than most Western destinations.

  • Medicine, engineering & business degrees
  • English-medium instruction from year one
  • Lower cost of living and tuition
